Privacy Policy
NutriKidney

Effective 13 July 2026 · NutriKidney for iOS · Codersize

NutriKidney does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What the app stores

NutriKidney helps you track phosphorus, potassium, sodium, and protein intake. To do this, it stores on your device:

  • Food entries you log (food, amount, date, meal type)
  • Your daily nutrient limits
  • Custom foods you create

This data is saved in a local database on your device only. It is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere by the app. The developer has no access to it.

What the app does not do

  • No account or registration
  • No internet connection is used — the app works entirely offline
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • No cookies, identifiers, or fingerprinting
  • No data shared with third parties — there are no third parties

Health information

Your logged food data is dietary information you choose to record for yourself. It stays on your device. NutriKidney is a tracking aid, not a medical device; always follow the dietary guidance of your healthcare provider.

Deleting your data

You can delete all logged data at any time in Settings → Reset Data, or by deleting the app, which removes its entire local database.

Diagnostics

The app itself sends nothing. If you have opted in to sharing diagnostics with Apple in iOS settings, Apple may provide the developer with anonymized crash reports under Apple's own privacy terms.

Children

NutriKidney does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If a future version of the app changes any of the above (for example, adding optional iCloud sync), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be described in the App Store release notes.
